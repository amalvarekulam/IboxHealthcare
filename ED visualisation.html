SDEC

SDEC is the provision of same day care for emergency patients who would otherwise be admitted to hospital.

Under this care model, patients presenting at hospital with relevant conditions can be rapidly assessed, diagnosed and treated without being admitted to a ward, and if clinically safe to do so, will go home the same day their care is provided.

Our SDEC dashboards provide a full overview of all the different clinical areas on one Live Status dashboards while allowing the clinical teams to manage patient care effectively

In an era where data silos impact patient care and safety IBOX extracts data from silos sources to create a virtual patient record helping patient flow, patient care and patient safety.

Our Award Winning solution enables clinicians to focus on the task of treating and looking after patients while the IBOX machine feeds them the required information at the ward, in the site office and on the go.
