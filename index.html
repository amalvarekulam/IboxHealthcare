Healthcare Partners

Providing a performance edge

Collaboration is key to transformational solutions. That’s why Zebra collaborates with partners in engaging with customers and delivering value. IBOX Healthcare is proud to be a Healthcare Zebra Partner and trusted to work with Zebra technology to transform patient care.

We provide software applications, specialised solutions and services that complement Zebra offerings. Being part of the award-winning Zebra® PartnerConnect programme, IBOX Healthcare grow, build greater expertise and serve customers more effectively. Together, we’re empowering customers by intelligently linking people, assets and data.

In an era where data silos impact patient care and safety IBOX extracts data from silos sources to create a virtual patient record helping patient flow, patient care and patient safety.

Our Award Winning solution enables clinicians to focus on the task of treating and looking after patients while the IBOX machine feeds them the required information at the ward, in the site office and on the go.
