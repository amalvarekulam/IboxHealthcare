About Us

Our Story

Since the start in 2010, IBOX Healthcare has grown within the healthcare sector as a trusted supplier of advanced patient pathway visualisation tools. Company Founder, Najum Khan, had the vision from day one that the need to connect disparate data silos was required in order to enhance patient safety, patient flow and patient experience. Today, IBOX Healthcare is an award winning platform that used across all areas of patient care within Northampton General Hospital from the Emergency Department to the Discharge HUB.

Our Team

A key goal for all healthcare providers is to improve the quality of care for patients and residents. Our integrated system gives you the ability to transform unreliable processes and inconsistent communications into a streamlined and centralised approach, revolutionising how you provide care.

IBOX Healthcare was founded by Najum Khan in 2010, with a vision of improving data visualisation and patient safety in healthcare. Combining human intuition with innovative methods through collaboration with public and private organisations across health and social care has made our risk, quality, and compliance software the only option for our partners across the world. Passion characterises our strategies, with the end goal always being to make healthcare safer. With our partners working alongside us, we can be sure that both their requirements and aspirations are not only met but exceeded.
